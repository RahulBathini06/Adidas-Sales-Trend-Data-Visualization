# Adidas US Sales Dashboard | Tableau Internship Project

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Adidas US Sales Dashboard** developed using **Tableau**. The dashboard analyzes Adidas sales data to identify trends, evaluate business performance, and provide actionable insights for decision-making.

The objective of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful visualizations that help stakeholders understand sales performance across different regions, products, retailers, and time periods.

## Business Objective

* Analyze overall sales performance.
* Identify top-performing products and retailers.
* Compare sales across regions and states.
* Track monthly and yearly sales trends.
* Evaluate profitability and operating margins.
* Support data-driven business decisions through interactive dashboards.

## Dashboard Features

* 📈 Sales Trend Visualization
* 💰 Total Sales and Profit Analysis
* 🌎 Region-wise and State-wise Sales Performance
* 🛍️ Product Category Analysis
* 🏪 Retailer Performance Analysis
* 📅 Time-Series Analysis (Monthly/Yearly)
* 🎛️ Interactive Filters and Dashboard Controls

## Tools & Technologies

* **Tableau Desktop**
* **Microsoft Excel**
* Data Visualization
* Business Analytics

## Key Insights

* Identified the highest revenue-generating regions.
* Compared retailer performance based on total sales.
* Highlighted the best-selling product categories.
* Analyzed monthly sales trends to identify seasonal demand.
* Evaluated operating profit and profit margins to measure business performance.
